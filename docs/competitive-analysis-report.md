# TeamSpark AI - Competitive Analysis Report

## Overview

AI-powered team communication and engagement platforms play a crucial role in modern team management[^1]. This report provides a detailed comparative analysis of major team engagement platforms, including 15Five, covering their features, pricing, and permission management.

## Major Platform Comparison

### 15Five

#### Key Features

15Five offers innovative AI-powered capabilities as a strategic performance management platform[^1][^2].

- **AI-Powered Predictive Impact Model**: Uses machine learning to identify the most effective areas for improving engagement[^2]
- **Continuous Performance Management**: Weekly check-ins, goal setting and tracking, 360-degree feedback[^3]
- **Engagement Measurement**: Customizable surveys and lifecycle surveys[^1]
- **Manager Effectiveness**: Real-time AI support and coaching features[^4]
- **Integrated Learning and Development**: Live and on-demand manager training[^4]


#### Pricing Structure

15Five offers five pricing plans[^5]:


| Plan Name | Price (Monthly/Employee) | Key Features |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Engage | \$4.00 | Engagement surveys and insights |
| Perform | \$10.00 | Performance management and feedback |
| Total Platform | \$16.00 | Engage + Perform, HR dashboard, learning courses |
| Transform Accelerator | \$220.00 (per manager) | Modern manager training |
| Manager Products | Contact for pricing | Data-driven manager coaching |

### Lattice

#### Key Features

Lattice provides comprehensive HR management solutions with a wide range of features[^6][^7].

- **Comprehensive Performance Management**: 360-degree reviews, goal setting, continuous feedback[^8]
- **Engagement Analytics**: Pulse surveys, eNPS, onboarding and exit surveys[^6]
- **Advanced Analytics**: Heat maps, sentiment tracking, trend comparisons[^6]
- **HRIS Integration**: HR information system integration[^7]
- **Compensation Management**: Salary benchmarking and compensation cycle management[^7]


#### Pricing Structure

Lattice offers flexible pricing[^9]:


| Plan Name | Price (Monthly/Employee) | Features |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Performance Management + OKRs | \$11.00 | Performance management and goal setting |
| Core HRIS | \$10.00 | Basic HR information system |
| Engagement | \$4.00 | Engagement measurement |
| Grow | \$4.00 | Growth and development support |
| Compensation | \$6.00 | Compensation management |

### Culture Amp

#### Key Features

Culture Amp provides specialized features as an employee experience platform[^10].

- **Data-Driven HR**: Comprehensive employee feedback collection and analysis[^10]
- **Integration Capabilities**: Connections with ADP, Slack, Microsoft Teams, HRIS systems[^10]
- **Personalized Growth Plans**: Individual development plans and learning opportunities[^10]
- **Multi-Language Support**: Multi-language support for global deployment[^10]


#### Pricing Structure

Culture Amp pricing varies by company size[^11]:

- Starting from \$4,500 annually
- Approximately \$27,000/year for 200 employees
- Up to \$45,000/year for 1,000 employees


### Qualtrics Employee Experience

#### Key Features

Qualtrics provides a comprehensive experience management platform[^12].

- **300+ Benchmark Items**: Comprehensive employee experience measurement[^12]
- **AI-Driven Insights**: Insights and recommended actions powered by generative AI[^12]
- **Predictive Engagement**: Identification of engagement factors and improvement strategies[^12]
- **Integrated Data Management**: Unified data management through Employee Experience ID[^13]


### Microsoft Viva Engage

#### Key Features

Microsoft Viva Engage specializes in communication and community features[^14].

- **Premium Community Features**: Branded employee apps[^14]
- **Centralized Campaign Management**: Unified communication strategy management[^14]
- **Leadership Corner**: Direct communication with executives[^14]


#### Pricing Structure

- Employee Communications and Communities: \$2.00/month (per user)
- Viva Suite: \$12.00/month (per user)[^14]


## Permission Management and Security Features

### Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Modern employee engagement platforms implement role-based access control as a standard[^15][^16].

#### Key Security Features

- **Principle of Least Privilege**: Granting minimum necessary access for job functions[^16]
- **Hierarchical Permission Management**: Permission hierarchy based on organizational structure[^16]
- **Automatic Provisioning**: Automatic permission updates when roles change[^15]


### Data Protection and Privacy

Employee data protection is a top priority[^17].

#### Essential Security Requirements

- **Data Encryption**: Encryption for both data in transit and at rest[^18]
- **GDPR Compliance**: Compliance with European General Data Protection Regulation[^19]
- **Audit Logs**: Recording all data access and modifications[^20]
- **SAML SSO Integration**: Secure single sign-on functionality[^21]


## Platform Selection Guidelines

### 15Five vs Lattice Comparison

The choice between 15Five and Lattice depends on organizational needs[^8][^4].

#### Organizations Suited for 15Five

- Small to medium businesses seeking a user-friendly platform[^8]
- Organizations focused on learning and development[^4]
- Companies with existing HRIS wanting to add performance management[^8]


#### Organizations Suited for Lattice

- Medium to large enterprises seeking comprehensive HR solutions[^8]
- US-based organizations requiring integrated HRIS and performance management[^8]
- Companies emphasizing engagement analytics and HR analytics[^8]


### Implementation Considerations

#### Technical Requirements

- **Integration Capabilities**: Compatibility with existing systems[^22]
- **Ease of Deployment**: Ability for rapid implementation[^22]
- **External Review Features**: 360-degree feedback including stakeholders[^22]


#### Organizational Requirements

- **Company Size**: Scalability appropriate for employee count[^11]
- **Industry Requirements**: Need for specific regulatory compliance[^17]
- **Budget Constraints**: Total cost of ownership considerations[^11]


## Recommendations

When selecting an employee engagement and evaluation system platform, we recommend comprehensive consideration of the following elements:

1. **Organization Size and Growth Plans**: Current employee count and future expansion plans
2. **Functional Requirements**: Priorities for performance management, engagement measurement, and learning development
3. **Technical Environment**: Integration requirements with existing systems and security standards
4. **Budget and ROI**: Balance between initial investment and long-term value creation
5. **User Experience**: Ease of use for employees and managers

As each platform has unique strengths, carefully evaluating the solution that best aligns with your organization's specific needs and strategic goals is key to success[^23].
